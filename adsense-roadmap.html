How to Get Approved in 2026?

Getting AdSense approval is like hitting a 150kg Deadlift—it requires patience, form, and consistency.

1. The Quality Content Rule

You need at least 15-20 high-quality articles. Don't just copy-paste; write about things you know, like mobile-first coding or fitness.

2. Mandatory Pages

Make sure your site has these pages live:

  • Privacy Policy (You already have this!)
  • About Us
  • Contact Us

3. Clean UI/UX

Your site must be mobile-friendly. Since we use Acode to build ONLYK, our code is lean and loads fast on all devices.
